## 1. Introduction
Welcome to WortWeg! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, use, and process your information when you use the WortWeg mobile application (the "App").

*Disclaimer: This app is currently in a Private Alpha phase for testing purposes only.*

## 2. Data We Collect and How We Use It

### 2.1. No Account Data (Anonymous Testing)
Currently, WortWeg does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide personal identification information (like your name or email address). You are testing the App completely anonymously.

### 2.2. Audio and Speech Data
The core feature of WortWeg involves speaking practice. To facilitate this:
- We request access to your device's microphone.
- When you use the speaking exercises, the audio is sent securely to our backend servers.
- The audio is then processed by third-party AI services (Azure Cognitive Services and/or OpenAI) solely to analyze your pronunciation, accuracy, and fluency.
- **We do not store raw audio files on our servers.** Once the analysis is complete and the score is returned to your device, the audio data is discarded by our backend. *(To verify before publishing: check retention policies of third-party APIs used)*.

### 2.3. AI Teacher Chat Data
If you interact with the AI Teacher feature:
- Your text inputs are sent to our backend and processed via Google Gemini and/or OpenAI.
- These inputs are processed anonymously for the sole purpose of generating educational feedback and corrections.

### 2.4. Local Progress Data
Your learning progress (such as completed lessons and XP) is stored locally on your device. We currently do not synchronize or back up this data to any cloud database. If you uninstall the app, your progress will be lost.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services to power WortWeg's educational features:
- **Microsoft Azure Cognitive Services:** Used for word-level pronunciation and fluency scoring.
- **OpenAI:** Used as a fallback for speech-to-text transcription and potential AI teacher features.
- **Google Gemini:** Used to power the AI Teacher text feedback.
These providers process your data strictly to provide the requested AI analysis and do not use your private interactions to train public models.

## 4. Payments
WortWeg is currently completely free to use during the Alpha phase. We do not collect any payment or financial information.
