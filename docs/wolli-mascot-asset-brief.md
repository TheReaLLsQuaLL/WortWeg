# Wolli Final Mascot Asset Brief

This brief defines a safe, original mascot direction for WortWeg. It is for future designer or image-generation work only.

Do not generate images from this document yet. Do not copy Stitch mascot art or competitor mascots.

## 1. Mascot Identity

- Name: Wolli
- Role: friendly German-learning guide for Turkish speakers.
- Personality:
  - warm
  - encouraging
  - clear
  - slightly playful
  - not childish
- Function:
  - explains grammar
  - gives practice tips
  - supports vocabulary review
  - supports mistakes review
  - supports speaking practice
  - helps the learner feel guided without dominating the screen

## 2. Visual Direction

- Species: original owl/bird-inspired guide, not sheep.
- Shape language:
  - soft rounded silhouette
  - readable at small mobile sizes
  - simple head/eyes/beak structure
  - expressive but not exaggerated
- Style:
  - 2D comic-book compatible
  - friendly mobile app mascot
  - premium and readable, not noisy or childish
- Must fit WortWeg UI:
  - bold 2px ink outlines
  - sticker-like shadow
  - lavender/white panels
  - yellow/purple accent system
  - subtle halftone allowed only as background/accent, not inside busy details
- Must work as:
  - small avatar
  - lesson guide
  - empty-state illustration
  - speaking/review helper

## 3. Avoid / Prohibited

Do not create or use:

- green owl mascot
- Duolingo-like proportions, expression, or colors
- sheep mascot
- wolf mascot
- cat mascot
- boy/person mascot
- copied Stitch-generated mascot
- copied competitor mascot silhouette
- official school/exam logos
- German eagle symbolism
- complex realistic bird illustration
- any copyrighted character similarity
- mascot art from external URLs

## 4. Color Guidance

Use an original palette compatible with WortWeg:

- lavender/purple accent
- warm yellow accent
- cream/white face or body areas
- optional teal or blue small accents
- dark ink outline compatible with the app comic system

Avoid:

- dominant bright green
- exact Duolingo-like green palette
- overly dark/serious colors
- noisy multicolor gradients
- colors that clash with the yellow CTA and purple selected states

## 5. Required Asset Set

Future asset pack should include:

- small avatar head
- full-body standing pose
- happy encouragement pose
- thinking/explaining pose
- speaking/microphone pose
- mistake/retry supportive pose
- empty-state sitting pose
- app icon candidate later, optional

Each pose should preserve the same Wolli silhouette and face language.

## 6. Mobile Constraints

Assets must be:

- readable at 32px
- readable at 48px
- readable at 96px
- available with transparent backgrounds
- compatible with light lavender and white UI surfaces
- compatible with future dark or deep-purple surfaces if needed
- simple enough to avoid visual clutter in dense screens
- free of tiny text inside the mascot
- built around a clear silhouette
- accessible in contrast against WortWeg paper/lavender panels

## 7. Prompt Guardrails For Future Image Generation

Reusable future prompt template:

```text

The style should fit a premium 2D comic-book mobile learning app with bold 2px ink outlines, a sticker-like shadow, lavender/purple and warm yellow accents, cream/white face or body areas, and a simple readable silhouette. The mascot must work as a small mobile avatar and as a lesson guide illustration.

Important restrictions: not a green owl, not Duolingo-style, not a sheep, not a cat, not a wolf, not a boy/person mascot, not based on Google Stitch mascot output, not copied from any competitor mascot, no official school or exam logos, no German eagle symbolism, no external URL assets, no copyrighted character similarity. Keep the design original, friendly, simple, and compatible with WortWeg's comic UI.
```

Pose-specific additions can be appended later, for example:

- `Pose: thinking/explaining, one wing raised like a teacher.`
- `Pose: speaking practice, holding or standing near a simple microphone icon.`
- `Pose: retry support, gentle encouraging expression, no shame or failure symbolism.`

Before accepting final mascot assets, confirm:

- original silhouette
- recognizable Wolli identity across all poses
- works in the current WortWeg comic UI
- no competitor resemblance
- no Duolingo-like green owl identity
- no sheep/wolf/cat/boy direction
- no copied Stitch-generated mascot traits
- no official exam/school claim
- no external URLs
- no copyrighted character similarity
- readable at small mobile sizes
- transparent background exports are available
